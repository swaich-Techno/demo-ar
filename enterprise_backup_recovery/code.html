Data Sovereignty Notice

NEON_ARCADE employs a strict local-first architecture. Backup shards are distributed solely across your authorized internal P2P network. No data is mirrored to external cloud servers. Loss of the Master Recovery Key and simultaneous failure of all authorized local nodes will result in permanent, unrecoverable data loss. Ensure physical or cold-storage backups of your recovery credentials.
